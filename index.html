// FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before engaging with us.

Why is OT security different from IT security?
+
OT environments have unique constraints — zero-downtime requirements, legacy equipment that cannot be patched, proprietary industrial protocols (Modbus, DNP3), and safety-critical systems where a breach can cause physical harm or operational shutdown. Standard IT security tools don't account for these and can actually disrupt operations if applied incorrectly to OT networks.
Will your assessments disrupt our OT operations?
+
No. We use passive, non-intrusive assessment methodologies specifically designed for OT environments where operational continuity is non-negotiable. Our team has extensive experience working in live railway, power, and manufacturing environments without causing any operational disruption whatsoever.
What is included in a Security Control Assessment?
+
Our Security Control Assessment evaluates effectiveness of your existing security controls — from policies and procedures to technical safeguards. Guided by IEC 62443-4-2, NIST CSF, and ISO 27002, you receive a comprehensive Security Controls Assessment Report as your definitive blueprint for confidence in your security posture.
Do you provide ongoing support after assessment?
+
Yes. We provide Remediation Support & Implementation services — hands-on guidance through implementation of every recommended measure. We don't just hand you a report and walk away. Our team stays engaged until every action item is implemented and verified.
Can you help us qualify for cyber insurance?
+
Absolutely. Our documentation, risk assessments, and audit reports are structured to meet cyber insurance requirements. Clients have qualified for coverage within 3 months, with premiums up to 35% lower due to strong documented security controls and evidence of continuous risk management.
Which industries do you specialize in?
+
We serve Railway/Metro (our core specialization), Manufacturing, Power & Energy, Healthcare, Oil & Gas, Government, IT/ITES, Aviation, and Banking. We have particularly deep expertise in OT/SCADA environments for critical infrastructure — where IT security firms lack the industrial systems knowledge to be truly effective.
