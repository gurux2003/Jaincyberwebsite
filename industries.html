OT/IT convergence creating new attack pathways into safety-critical systems

Oil & Gas

Oil and gas infrastructure presents some of the highest cyber-physical risk anywhere. A successful attack on pipeline SCADA or refinery control systems can cause environmental disasters, explosions, and safety incidents with catastrophic consequences beyond just financial loss.

Key Security Challenges
Remote and geographically dispersed pipeline and wellhead SCADA systems
Safety instrumented systems (SIS) that must never be disrupted or tampered with
Nation-state actors specifically targeting energy infrastructure for geopolitical leverage
